About This Site

This website hosts a collection of articles from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology on topics that I personally find interesting. Take a look, and if you're interested in any of them, please feel free to read the full article!

Why These Feeds?

I chose each of these feeds for a specific reason. They are either of pure interest to me, I think they are important topics, and some are a bit of both. Climate Change is something everyone is dealing with, and I find the ways people around the globe are trying to combat it is fascinating. Neuroscience is a topic that's peeked my interest for years, and developments in the field are intriguing. Music is something I simply just like. All and any types really. History is a very broad topic, making the rabbit holes you can fall down almost infinite.

The hope for this site is to share these articles in a user-friendly manner, letting you browse at your leisure and find information that I think is important or just interesting.

Explore the feeds using the navigation bar above. More feeds may be added over time.
